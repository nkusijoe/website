Our Story

What you can know about us.

Our Objective

to contribute in having a healed community through creating a safe environment where anyone can be able to open up, be supported, offered hope and courage to carry on in this life.

Our Activities

Safe place activities include, but not limited to;
*Counseling:Children,individuals,couples and people living with disabilities
*Community-based activities and prevention:Outreach,Schools,Orphanages and geriatrics.
*Research and development:Trainings and workshops
*Advocacy and empowerment:Exchange and awareness platforms.

Our Goal

By 2025, at least 5000 people should have access to quality, safe and affordable mental health services

Our Approach

We believe in having a healed community and that’s exactly the driving force of SAFE PLACE in all its endeavors. To achieve that we adopted a range of approaches; these are behavioral therapy, cognitive therapy, interpersonal therapy, psychoanalysis, psychodynamic psychotherapy, supportive psychotherapy.

Source Of Fund

SAFE PLACE is for-non-profit and runs its activities from the support freely given by benevolent donors and other parties who are interested in the cause reflected in our objective

Beneficiaries

The beneficiaries are those who need our services as reflective in the objective irrespective of their gender, age, religion or other affiliations. These include, but not limited to, children, youth, adults, couples and people living with disabilities.

Managment

All the activities referred to herein are assigned to people who are committed to this cause with their expertise and goodwill. These includes, but not limited to, the founder, the executive committee, the SAFE PLACE community and all other parties involved in carrying out all SAFE PLACE activities.
