About Us

At safe place we understand when human beings experience trauma or severe life stressors, it is not uncommon for their lives to unravel.Our great passion is bringing healing to people who have been through a traumatic/stressful experience.
We help people, who include Children, adults and families, to find healthy perceptions of themselves and strengthen their relationships so they can know themselves as peaceful, complete, whole and safe.

We can only archive this through praying, listening and providing a safe place for everyone to feel accepted, loved, understood and cared for.
We know that no single approach is the right one for every individual, and so we have been purposed ourselves to listen and develop both personalized and group approaches towards healing.

Mission

Providing quality, affordable and accessible mental health services.

Vision

To become an excellent, safe and reliable organization that provides sustainable ways of contributing in having a mentally healthy community.
